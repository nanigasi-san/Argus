ARGUS Privacy Policy

ARGUS is a geofencing support app. This policy explains what data the app uses and how that data is handled.

1. Data used by the app

  • Location data for foreground and background geofence monitoring.
  • Camera access to scan GeoJSON QR codes.
  • Local files selected by the user to import GeoJSON data.
  • Notification permission to warn the user when they leave the configured area.

2. How the data is handled

  • Location data is processed on the device to evaluate geofence status.
  • Camera input is processed on the device to decode QR codes.
  • Imported GeoJSON and temporary restore files remain on the device.
  • No developer-operated external server receives location, camera, or GeoJSON data.

3. Third-party services

ARGUS is built with Flutter and may rely on platform components provided by Android, Google Play services, and ML Kit for barcode scanning. Their handling of data is governed by their own terms and privacy policies.

4. Third-party sharing

The developer does not sell, share, or provide personal data to third parties.

5. Data retention

Data used by ARGUS remains on the device unless the user removes the app or deletes the files.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent through the ARGUS issue tracker.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated when the app or legal requirements change.
